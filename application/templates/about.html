Our Story

LearnHack tutoring was created in 2019 after brothers Harrison and Charlie recognised the increasing demand for coders in the workplace.

We are an online tutoring business that offer the best tutors (Here at LearnHack we call them ProfHackers), at the best times, at the best rates. We seek out the most engaging ProfHackers, who are passionate about their field, love to teach and have the qualifications to back it all up.

We believe that today's online world deserves a coding agency that suits your life. One that allows you to learn in your own time and in your own home.

We're here for those who want to learn coding. Who want to better themselves in the skill of the future. Who want to have fun.
